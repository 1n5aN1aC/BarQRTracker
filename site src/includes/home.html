MyDegrees: one student's experience


View on YouTube

MyDegrees is an exciting new tool to help students plan for their academic goals and track their progress throughout their OSU experience. It is used for course planning, advising, and degree verfication. The system was used by advisors in the undergraduate programs during Fall and Winter Terms 2010/11 and was made available to undergraduate students beginnning April 12, 2011. To access MyDegrees, select it on the Student Records menu within On-line Services. You can login to Online Services by clicking here.

Select the links on the right of this page to find out more about the system and the project to implement it. By selecting "Learning about the system", you will find resources to help students and advisors learn how to use MyDegrees.
